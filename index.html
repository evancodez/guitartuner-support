Guitar Tuner Support

Fast, accurate chromatic tuner for iPhone. This page provides help, tips, and contact.

How to Use

  1. Open the app and allow Microphone when prompted.
  2. Pluck a single string close to your iPhone’s mic. The needle shows ±cents and the center note (e.g., A2).
  3. Adjust A4 reference (415–466 Hz) if you need non-standard tuning.

Tip: Noisy rooms can confuse detection—move closer to the mic and mute other strings.

FAQ

The needle isn’t moving.

Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone and make sure Guitar Tuner is ON. Force-quit and relaunch the app. Try plucking a louder note near the mic.

Does the app record or send audio?

No. Pitch is detected on-device only. We don’t collect personal data.

How do I change A4?

Use the A4 slider (415–466 Hz). The cents and target notes update immediately.
